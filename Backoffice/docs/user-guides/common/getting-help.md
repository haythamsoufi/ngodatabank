# Getting help

Use this page if you are stuck and need help.

## First, try these quick checks

- **Refresh the page** and try again (temporary issues can happen).
- Make sure you are in the **right country** (if your work depends on country access).
- Check your **internet connection**.

## If you can’t see something you expected

- If you are a focal point and **don’t see assignments**, you may not be assigned to a country yet.
- If you can log in but **don’t see the right menu items**, your role/permissions may be limited.

## Where to find guidance

- **Start here**: see [How the platform works](../../getting-started/how-it-works.md)
- **Definitions**: see [Glossary](../../getting-started/glossary.md)
- **Step-by-step guides**: see [User guides](../README.md)

## How to ask for support (recommended information)

When contacting support, include:

- What you were trying to do (1–2 sentences)
- The page you were on (copy the URL if possible)
- Your role (admin or focal point)
- Your country/countries (if relevant)
- A screenshot of the message/error
- The time it happened
