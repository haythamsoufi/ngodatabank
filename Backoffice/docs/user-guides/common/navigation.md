# Navigation basics

This guide explains the main areas of the platform and how to find common pages.

## Main places you’ll use

### Dashboard (all users)

Your home page. It typically shows your pending work and quick links.

### Account settings (all users)

Use **Account settings** to update your profile, change your password, and select your preferred language.

## If you are a focal point

- **My assignments**: you’ll see assignments on your dashboard.
- **Data entry**: open an assignment to fill in the form.

## If you are an admin

Common areas in the **Admin** panel:

- **User Management → Manage Users**: create and update accounts and country access.
- **Form & Data Management → Manage Templates**: create and maintain form templates.
- **Form & Data Management → Manage Assignments**: distribute templates to countries and track progress.

## Tips

- Use the sidebar to move between sections.
- If you can’t find a page, use the platform search (if enabled) or ask your administrator.
