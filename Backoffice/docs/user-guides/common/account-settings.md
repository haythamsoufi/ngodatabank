# Account settings

Use this page to update your profile and preferences.

## What you can update

- **Profile**: name, title (job title/role)
- **Profile Color**: choose a color for your profile icon
- **AI Chatbot**: enable or disable the AI chatbot assistant (if available)
- **Registered Devices**: view and manage your registered mobile devices for push notifications

## What you cannot change here

- **Email address**: Your email is managed by SSO and cannot be changed. Contact your system administrator.
- **Password**: Password changes must be done by a system administrator. Contact your administrator to change your password.
- **Roles**: Your roles are managed by system administrators.

## Update your profile

1. Open **Account settings** (from your dashboard or user menu).
2. Update your **Name** and **Title** (optional).
3. Choose a **Profile Color** for your profile icon.
4. If available, toggle **AI Chatbot** on or off.
5. Click **Save changes**.

## Manage registered devices

You can view and manage your registered mobile devices:

- **View devices**: See all devices registered for push notifications
- **End session**: Log out from a specific device (keeps device registered)
- **Remove device**: Permanently remove a device from the registry

**Note**: To change your email address or password, please contact your system administrator.

## Tips

- If you change your language, some pages may still appear in English until translated.
- Keep your contact information up to date.
