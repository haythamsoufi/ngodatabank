# View Your Assignments

Your dashboard shows the forms assigned to you (and their status).

## Before you start

- You need **Focal point** access (or an admin acting as a focal point).
- You must be assigned to at least one **country**.

## Steps

1. Open your **Dashboard**.
2. Review your assignment cards (title, deadline, and status).
3. Click an assignment to open the form.

## Understanding Assignment Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Not started yet |
| **In Progress** | Started but not submitted |
| **Submitted** | Completed and submitted |
| **Overdue** | Past deadline, not submitted |

## Tips

- Start early to avoid last‑minute issues.
- Your progress is usually **saved automatically** as you work.
- If you don’t see an expected assignment, contact your administrator.
