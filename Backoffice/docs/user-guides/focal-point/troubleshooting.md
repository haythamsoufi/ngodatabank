# Troubleshooting (Focal point)

Use this page if you are trying to complete an assignment and something is not working.

## I can’t see any assignments

Possible reasons:

- You are **not assigned to any country** yet.
- There are **no active assignments** for your country/countries.

What to do:

1. Confirm you can see your **country/countries** (if your organization uses country access).
2. If you think you should have assignments, contact your administrator and ask them to:
   - confirm your **role** is focal point (data entry)
   - confirm your **country assignments**
   - confirm there is an assignment created for that country

## I can open the form, but I can’t submit

Common causes:

- A **required field** is missing.
- There is a **validation message** (for example: an invalid number format).
- The assignment is **locked** because it is already submitted/approved (workflow dependent).

What to do:

1. Scroll through the form sections and look for highlighted fields or messages.
2. Fill in missing required fields (marked with * if shown).
3. Fix validation messages.
4. Try **Submit** again.

For more detail, see: [Troubleshooting submission issues](troubleshooting-submission.md)

## My progress is missing

Most forms save automatically, but it can depend on the page and connection.

What to do:

- If you see **Save draft**, click it before leaving the page.
- Avoid closing the tab immediately after major edits—wait a few seconds.
- If you lost work, contact support and share:
  - assignment name
  - when you last saw the saved data
  - what fields/sections were affected

## I see “Access denied”

This usually means you do not have permission for that page or country.

What to do:

- Contact your administrator and ask them to review your **role** and **country access**.
