# Supporting documents (focal point)

Use this guide if your assignment asks you to upload evidence or source files alongside the form.

## Before you start

- Confirm what documents are required for your assignment (if any).
- Make sure your files are ready (and not password-protected unless required).

## Upload documents

1. Open your assignment from the **Dashboard**.
2. Look for a section like **Documents**, **Attachments**, or **Supporting documents**.
3. Click **Upload** (or **Add file**).
4. Select your file and confirm.
5. Repeat for any additional documents.

## Tips

- Use clear file names (example: `Country_Period_Source.pdf`).
- If a document is large, upload it first (so you don’t lose time at the end).
- Don’t upload sensitive personal data unless your organization explicitly requires it.
- If you are asked to upload a spreadsheet, avoid extra tabs/hidden sheets unless needed (keep it simple).

## Common problems

- **I can’t see the upload area**: you may not have the right role to upload documents—ask your admin.
- **Upload fails**: try a smaller file, check your internet connection, or use a different file format.
- **I’m not sure what to upload**: re-read the assignment instructions and ask your admin what “required evidence” means for this assignment.
