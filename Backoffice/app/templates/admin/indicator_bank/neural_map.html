{{ _('Why do similar indicators sometimes appear far apart?') }}

{{ _('Embeddings live in a 1,536-dimensional space. Projecting that down to 2 or 3 dimensions inevitably loses information — like flattening a globe into a 2D map where some distances get distorted. The similarity scores shown in the detail panel are always calculated in the full-dimensional space, so they are accurate regardless of where dots appear on screen.') }}
